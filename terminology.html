Terminology

Validation state

  • Invalid
  • Complete: filled out and accurate
  • Untouched
  • Partially complete? (may be considered invalid or untouched)

Areas of work

  • Section: pages in the top-level navigation
  • Subsection: pages in the second and third-level navigation
  • Field set: multiple related fields (area code, first three, and last four digits of phone number)
    • They can be nested (address relating to a person)
    • They can be nested and nested again (address relating to a person in a list of people)
