eApp Design/Experience Principles

Design principles are concise, specific guidelines for generating and evaluating ideas and artifacts. The goal of these principles are to give the team a shared language to describe the product and evaluation criteria for features. This is meant to cover design principles specific to eApp, rather than general best practices.
